# Case Study: Resource Stack Adoption

## Scenario
A small research team had no standard pre-submission quality process.

## Stack adopted
- submission readiness checklist
- reporting standards mapping
- citation integrity pass
- revision tracker workflow

## Result
- cleaner internal review process
- fewer late-stage rewrites
- better role clarity across team members
